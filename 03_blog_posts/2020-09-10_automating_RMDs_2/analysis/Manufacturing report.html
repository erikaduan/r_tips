Labour force analysis by industry

Manufacturing

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 921,087 individuals were employed in Manufacturing in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 852,760 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is a decrease of 7.42 percentage points since Feb 2020.
