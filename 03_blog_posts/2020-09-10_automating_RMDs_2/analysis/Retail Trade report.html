Labour force analysis by industry

Retail Trade

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 1,244,774 individuals were employed in Retail Trade in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 1,189,095 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is a decrease of 4.47 percentage points since Feb 2020.
