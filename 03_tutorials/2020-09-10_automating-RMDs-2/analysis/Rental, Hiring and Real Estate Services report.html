Labour force analysis by industry

Rental, Hiring and Real Estate Services

Last updated on 2020-09-19.


A total of 219,271 individuals were employed in Rental, Hiring and Real Estate Services in Feb 2020, before the onset of COVID-19 spread in Australia.

A total of 220,576 individuals were employed in the most recent quarter of May 2020, which is an increase of 0.6 percentage points since Feb 2020.
